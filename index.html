Heritage

In the heart of Riyadh, Queen Anne was born from the city itself—bold, elegant, and full of contrast. Inspired by the spirit of the Queen Anne style—a little classic, a little dramatic, always full of detail—we imagined a space that brings people together. Not just for coffee, but for the feeling of being seen, connected, and a little inspired.


Our café is made for those who notice the little things—the curve of a chair, the color of light at 4PM, the scent of a warm pastry with good coffee. We don’t believe in rushing. We believe in mood. In beauty. In stories shared over slow drinks. We are not minimal. We are meaningful.


Welcome to Queen Anne. Your place in the city.
